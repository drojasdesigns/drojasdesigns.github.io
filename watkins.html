J.R. Watkins Postcard Invitation

This was a client that asked for a more high-class invitation that reflected the Watkins brand since the invitations for the home parties are not provided by the company and must be made by the hosts themselves. The idea was an oversized postcard that could be mailed or given out. The background is a chalkboard, a throwback to the way the brand has distinguished itself as natural or handmande. The middle of each side features the recognizable art noveau style piece that adorns each product, done in chalk.
