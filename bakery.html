B's Bakery

B’s Bakery is an up and coming bakery shop that wants to go beyond the rest in quality and customer service. Everything revolves around identifying the brand through the B’s and cream swirl. A pattern made of inverted swirls adorns almost all the aspects of the system except for the stickers, as well as defining the shape of the envelope flap and letterhead. Four different pastel colors, associated with what kinds of treats are in the box, are also options for the different colors of the stationary system, allowing for matching or mismatching. Currently a work in progress.
