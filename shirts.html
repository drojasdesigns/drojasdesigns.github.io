Jeffrey Jacobs T-Shirt

The T-shirt design seen was based on the letterpress design and relief printmaking, as well as band posters announcing new tours. The design was made by hand-inking the ends of freshly cut 2x4, pressing them down to make the impressions that are seen. The pattern was later worked out to be able to draw the eye through the words and bring you to all of the most important information.

Fresh T-Shirt

This was inspired by 70’s shirt designs and old farm advertisements and is a parody on the hip-hop use of the word “Fresh”. In bboying, many dancers wear colors that will make them stand out among the crowd, and make people remember them. The bright orange is a parody on this fashion, and the design a throwback to the 70’s when it all started. This was screenprinted by hand and had an edition of 15.
