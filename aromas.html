Aroma's Rebrand Logo and Coffee Packaging

This is packaging for coffee that was based on a local coffeehouse called Aromas. They have many varieties of coffee. To showcase this, the color is different for each bag of coffee. This is also to allow identification based on a quick glance. The sides of the coffee bag have the main flavor on the sides of the coffee so that the bags can be put sideways to save room and still be easily identified. The bottom swirl shows part of the logo leading to the name.
