Juxtapoz Magazine Layout and Masthead Redesign

Focusing on the flowing work of Audrey Kawasaki to create the spaces that the rigid body copy could juxtapose against, was the prime moving force behind this piece. The color changes in the interview shows who is talking, with her work engaging you throughout the interview. The cover uses a frosted sheet of vellum, masking the face of one of her pieces with her name and the name of the magazine.
